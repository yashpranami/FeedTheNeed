FEED THE NEED

Hey there ! We believe in living for today and tomorrow, and so our work does the same. We help poor and needy people fighting poverty, starvation, medication and some other basic things. Here, we do so by partnering with various Restraunts, organisations, hotels, any domestic place or any other places where we can find food or things which can be helpful for poor and needy people. We grow together when we help each other together, and that's another way of giving a treat to yourself by helping others. We provide you with some rewards which can be redeemed afterwards.. So, stop thinking and join us, let's continue the chain of kindness and welfare as a society.

How you can help -

Our decisions have a lasting impact, we make them thoughtfully. Our mission has been to help poor and needy. You can help us by contributing your wishful amount or by simply contacting us for giving food or any type of basic things which can be helpful for a needy in any way. Another way of helpful deed you can do is by promoting us to your contacts and being a responsible part of this society.

Become an agent of change

We design interventions to ensure reliable meals for children in underserved communities where food can act as an incentive to education and skill development. We support Slum Schools, Skill Development centres, Creches, Community Development Centres and Child Shelter Homes.
